Matching Game




Timer:


Welcom to my matching game!
The theme of the game is: "Things you can't do during a pandemic". The goal is to match all the pairs before the time runs out. The amount of time given is based on the number of pairs selected. When the game starts players are given a short period of time to memorize the card placement. When matching cards are selected they are removed from the game board. When all cards are matched or the time runs out the game ends.
The score is calculated by adding the time remaining on the counter when a match is found. So the faster you answer the higher your score!
